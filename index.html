Quarter 1 · January – March
Q1 – Visibility
“You cannot fix what you cannot see.”
Focused on uncovering hidden systems and unseen compliance gaps, especially as population and complexity increase.
January
Visibility
Prospects
State outreach – Visibility launch for all regions & population tiers. A/B on “blind spots” messaging + discovery load.
Service Providers
How to submit clean reports – educate contractors & ITM companies on BRYCER-first workflows.
Customer Upsell
Monthly upsell theme (Fire Ext / RR / Multi-System) aligned to “see everything in one place.”
February
Visibility
Prospects
Continue Q1 Visibility A/B – resend winning variant + continue state outreach across regions and tiers.
Service Providers
No dedicated SP campaign – leverage January content in SDR & AM conversations with contractors.
Customer Upsell
Monthly upsell – position BRYCER as the single source of truth for fire and life-safety systems.
March
Visibility
Prospects
Final Q1 Visibility outreach plus Q1 VISIBILITY drips to move leads into Q2.
Service Providers
How to reduce rejections – align contractors to AHJ requirements and reduce manual back-and-forth.
Customer Upsell
Monthly upsell mapped to visibility outcomes (more systems actively tracked).
Customer Newsletter
Q1 newsletter – product updates, training, case studies focused on visibility & discovery load.
Q1 Drip Campaigns – Visibility
January: No drip – focus on state outreach only (region × population).
February: No drip – continue state outreach and A/B testing.
March: Cold, Warm, and High Intent Visibility drips all run in Weeks 10–12 (last 2–3 weeks of the quarter).
All three drips close out Q1 and move engaged and non-engaged leads into Q2 Efficiency messaging.
Quarter 2 · April – June
Q2 – Efficiency

Efficiency
Prospects
Launch Q2 Efficiency outreach – A/B emails by region & tier focused on admin overload and manual review.
Service Providers
No new SP campaign – reference Q1 content when discussing rejection reduction and cleaner submissions.
Customer Upsell
Monthly upsell – spotlight Report Reviewer upgrade and automation value.
May
Efficiency
Prospects
Continue Q2 A/B + resend winners; highlight reduced admin hours and inspection backlog stories.
Service Providers
Clear requirements improve performance – show contractors how clarity in BRYCER reduces delays.
Customer Upsell
Monthly upsell – efficiency-focused message (do more with the same team).
June
Efficiency
Prospects
Wrap Q2 outreach plus Q2 EFFICIENCY drips at the end of the quarter.
Service Providers
No new SP campaign – share May content in AM/SDR follow-ups with key contractors.
Customer Upsell
Monthly upsell around automation and freeing up internal admin resources.
Customer Newsletter
Q2 newsletter – efficiency wins, RR impact, and “give inspectors their time back” narrative.
Q2 Drip Campaigns – Efficiency
April: No drip – focus on Q2 Efficiency state outreach and launch.
May: No drip – continue outreach and A/B, build audience for end-of-quarter drips.
June: Cold, Warm, and High Intent Efficiency drips all run in Weeks 10–12 (last 2–3 weeks of June).
All drips fire at the end of June, reinforcing the “remove administrative burden” story and setting up Q3 Compliance.
